HOW TO SHOW COMPASSION

13 May, 2018 By Mathew Johnson

TRUSTING GOD IN EVERY SEASON

Children are the purest gift of life. Their laughter brings light to even the darkest days, and their curiosity reminds us how beautiful the world can be. Every child holds endless dreams within their small hearts — dreams of love, adventure, and happiness. It is our responsibility to guide them with kindness and teach them the values that will help them grow into strong and compassionate people.

When we spend time with children, we learn again how to see the world with wonder. They teach us patience, honesty, and the importance of simple joys. A child’s love is sincere and unconditional; they don’t care about wealth or status — they only understand warmth and care.

Every child

Every child deserves a safe home, good education, and people who believe in them. If we give them these things, they will build a better future for all of us. Children are not just the future — they are the hope and heart of the world today.

Children are the heart of every nation and the light of every home. They fill the world with laughter, dreams, and endless imagination. When we look into a child’s eyes, we can see honesty, hope, and a world full of possibilities. They are not only our future but also our greatest teachers. Through their innocence, we remember how to love without fear, to forgive quickly, and to find joy in the smallest things.

A child’s mind is like fresh soil, ready to grow whatever we plant in it. If we plant love, respect, and knowledge, we will see kindness, wisdom, and strength bloom. That is why it is so important to give children time, care, and attention. Every word we say and every action we take becomes a lesson for them.

Children DESERVE a WORLD

Children deserve a world where they can play freely, learn safely, and dream without limits. It is our duty to protect them, guide them, and believe in their potential. Because when a child smiles, the whole world becomes a little brighter, and when a child suffers, the whole world loses a piece of its beauty.

  • Children are the most precious gift in the world
  • They bring happiness and joy to every family.
  • Their smiles can make even a sad day brighter.

Children teach us many things without even trying. Through their eyes, we can see a world full of wonder and beauty. They remind us to smile more, forgive easily, and enjoy the simple things in life. A child’s laughter can fill a home with peace and joy, making even the hardest day feel lighter.
