OUR MISSIOn & Vision

BRINgING PEACE AND JOY
TO THE WORLD

Every living being depends on nature. Trees produce oxygen and clean the air. Rivers and oceans provide us with water. The sun gives us warmth and light. Animals and plants help keep the balance of the environment. But today, people are cutting down forests, polluting the air, and wasting water. Because of this, the Earth is getting warmer, and many animals are losing their homes.
